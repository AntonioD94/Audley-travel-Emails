The Official Stuff

Here’s a quick check list of the official documents and processes that need to be in order for your trip.

  • ☑ A valid passport. Your passport must be valid for the entire duration of your stay in Aruba.
  • ☑ A completed online ED card for every traveler (yes, even kids and babies).
  • ☑ Your Aruba Visitor Insurance must be purchased during the ED Card process.

Check these three boxes and you’ll be good to go!
